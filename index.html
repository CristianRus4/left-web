How Left Works

Are widgets free, or do I need to upgrade?

Left is free to try with essentials (special dates, a custom lifespan, and core styles). You can unlock every widget style, color theme, and advanced options with a one‑time, pay‑what‑you‑want purchase. Choose from three prices — regardless of what you choose, everything unlocks.

How do I add a widget to my iPhone’s Home Screen?
  1. Touch and hold the Home Screen until apps jiggle.
  2. Tap + (top‑left) and search for Left.
  3. Pick a style and size (small, medium, large).
  4. Tap Add Widget, then long‑press the widget and choose Edit Widget to configure it.
How do I add a widget to the Lock Screen?
  1. Touch and hold the Lock Screen and tap Customize.
  2. Tap the widget area and choose Left.
  3. Select the widget type you want and finish customization.

StandBy widgets can be added from the same customization flow.

How can I change the widget size?

Size is chosen when adding a widget. In the system picker, swipe to select Small, Medium, or Large, then tap Add Widget. You can long‑press the widget later to edit its settings and style.

How do I add widgets on iPad or Mac?

iPad: touch and hold the Home Screen → tap + → search for Left → choose size → Add Widget.

Mac: right‑click the desktop and choose Edit Widgets (or open Notification Center and click Edit Widgets) → search for Left → drag a widget into place.

Left doesn’t appear in my widget list
  1. Open the Left app at least once after installing.
  2. Update to the latest iOS/iPadOS/macOS and the latest app version.
  3. Restart your device and try adding the widget again.
Why don’t widgets refresh more often?

iOS manages widget refreshes to save battery. Apps request updates, but the system decides when they occur. Opening Left updates widgets immediately; otherwise, refreshes happen on a schedule within the system’s budget.

What devices are compatible?

Left is ready for iOS 26 and works on all compatible iPhones. You can also add widgets on iPad and Mac.

How many widget types are available?

Over 15 widget styles in multiple sizes, six color themes, and a range of symbols — each widget is fully customizable.

How is life expectancy calculated?

Life expectancy is estimated using Apple Intelligence and multiple factors such as age, gender, activity, sleep, country of birth, and more.

Does it work offline and is my data private?

Yes. Widgets work offline and data stays on your device. See our Privacy page for details.

What is a Year Progress widget?

A Year Progress widget shows how much of the current year has passed (or is left). It’s perfect for planning, reviews, and staying motivated across long‑term goals.

Is Left a time left app?

Yes. Left is a time left app that visualizes the remaining time for days, weeks, months, years, and your lifetime — plus countdowns for events and habits.
