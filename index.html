FAQ

How do I add a Countdown widget to my iPhone Home Screen?
  1. Touch and hold the Home Screen until apps jiggle.
  2. Tap the + button in the top-left corner.
  3. Search for Left and choose a widget style/size.
  4. Tap Add Widget, then configure the event or style.

You can also add Lock Screen and StandBy widgets from the same flow.

What’s the price?

Left is free to try with core features like special dates and a custom lifespan. Unlock everything with a one‑time, pay‑what‑you‑want purchase — choose from three prices and get the full app no matter what you pay.

What devices are compatible?

Left works on all iPhones running iOS 18 and iOS 26. You can also add widgets on iPad and Mac.

How many widgets are available?

There are over 15 unique widgets in small, medium, and large sizes, with six color themes and a variety of symbols. Every widget is fully customizable to fit your style.

How is life expectancy calculated?

Life expectancy is estimated using Apple Intelligence and multiple factors, including age, gender, exercise and activity patterns, sleep, country of birth, and more.

Does Left support Lock Screen and StandBy?

Yes. Left includes interactive widgets for the Home Screen, Lock Screen, and StandBy.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Your events and widgets continue to work offline; network is only needed for updates and optional services.

What about privacy?

Left is private by design. Data stays on your device. See our Privacy page for details.
